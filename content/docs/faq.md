# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Eziox.

## General

### What is Eziox?

Eziox is a modern bio link platform that lets you create a personalized page with all your important links in one place. Share one link instead of many.

### Is Eziox free?

Yes! Eziox Core is completely free with unlimited links, all themes, full analytics, and more. Premium tiers offer additional customization options.

### How is Eziox different from Linktree?

- **No paywalls on core features** - All themes and analytics are free
- **Modern design** - 31+ themes with animated backgrounds
- **Better privacy** - No third-party tracking, GDPR compliant
- **Open development** - Transparent about our features and roadmap

### What's my bio page URL?

Your bio page is at `eziox.link/yourusername`. You can also use short links at `eziox.link/s/code`.

## Account

### How do I change my username?

1. Go to **Dashboard → Profile**
2. Edit the **Username** field
3. Click **Save Changes**

Note: Your old username becomes available for others immediately.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Danger Zone**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm with your password

All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

### I forgot my password

1. Go to [Sign In](/sign-in)
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for a reset link

### Why can't I log in?

Common reasons:

- **Wrong password** - Try the password reset
- **Account locked** - Wait 30 minutes after 5 failed attempts
- **2FA required** - Enter your authenticator code
- **Email not verified** - Check your inbox for verification email

## Links

### How many links can I add?

Unlimited! There's no limit on the number of links.

### Can I reorder my links?

Yes! Drag and drop links in the Dashboard to reorder them.

### Can I schedule links?

Yes! Set start and end dates for links to appear/disappear automatically. Available for all users.

### Can I track link clicks?

Yes! Every link has detailed analytics including:

- Total clicks
- Device breakdown
- Geographic data
- Click heatmap by hour

## Customization

### How do I change my theme?

1. Go to **Dashboard → Customization**
2. Browse the theme gallery
3. Click a theme to preview
4. Click **Apply** to save

### Can I use custom colors?

Yes! Use the Theme Builder to create custom color schemes, or use Custom CSS (Pro+) for full control.

### Can I use my own domain?

Yes, with Creator tier! Add your domain in Dashboard → Creator and configure DNS.

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?

- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 90+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

### Is there a mobile app?

Not yet, but our website is fully mobile-optimized. A PWA is planned for the future.

### Do you have an API?

Yes! Generate API keys in Dashboard → API Access. See our [API Documentation](/docs/api).

### Is my data backed up?

Yes, we use Neon PostgreSQL with automatic backups. Pro+ users can also create manual backups.
