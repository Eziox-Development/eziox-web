# Analytics Dashboard

Understand your audience and optimize your bio page with Eziox Analytics.

## Overview

Access your analytics at [eziox.link/analytics](/analytics) or from your Dashboard.

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Profile Views** | Total visits to your bio page |
| **Link Clicks** | Total clicks across all links |
| **Click Rate** | Clicks รท Views (engagement rate) |
| **Followers** | Users following your profile |

## Time Ranges

View your data across different periods:

- **7 Days** - Recent activity
- **30 Days** - Monthly overview (default)
- **90 Days** - Quarterly trends
- **1 Year** - Long-term growth

## Charts & Visualizations

### Daily Activity Chart

Interactive bar chart showing:
- Daily profile views (blue bars)
- Daily link clicks (purple bars)
- Hover for exact numbers

### Top Performing Links

See which links get the most engagement:
- Link title and URL
- Click count
- Percentage of total clicks
- Click trend (up/down)

### Traffic Sources

Understand where your visitors come from:
- Direct traffic
- Social media referrals
- Search engines
- Other websites

## Link Analytics

Each link has its own detailed analytics:

### Per-Link Metrics

- **Total Clicks** - Lifetime click count
- **Unique Visitors** - Deduplicated by session
- **Click Heatmap** - Clicks by hour (0-23)

### Device Breakdown

- Desktop vs Mobile vs Tablet
- Browser distribution (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
- Operating system (Windows, macOS, iOS, Android)

### Geographic Data

- Country distribution
- City-level data (anonymized)
- Regional trends

## Export Data

**Pro+ Feature**

Export your analytics in multiple formats:

- **CSV** - Spreadsheet-compatible
- **JSON** - Developer-friendly

Export includes:
- Daily view/click counts
- Link performance data
- Traffic source breakdown
- Device statistics

## Privacy & Data

We take privacy seriously:

- **IP Anonymization** - Last octet zeroed (GDPR compliant)
- **No Third-Party Tracking** - We don't use Google Analytics
- **Data Retention** - 30 days for Free, unlimited for Pro+
- **Self-Service Export** - Download all your data anytime

## Tips for Growth

1. **Post consistently** - Share your bio link regularly
2. **Optimize link order** - Put important links at the top
3. **Use compelling titles** - Clear, action-oriented text
4. **Track campaigns** - Use UTM parameters for marketing
5. **A/B test** - Try different link titles and thumbnails
